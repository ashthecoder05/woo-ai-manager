# Social Media Post Templates — Bitcoin Commerce

## Platform Limits
| Platform | Limit | Optimal |
|----------|-------|---------|
| X (Twitter) | 280 chars | 180–220 chars |
| LinkedIn | 3000 chars | 150–300 chars |
| Instagram caption | 2200 chars | 100–200 chars |
| Mastodon | 500 chars | 200–350 chars |

## Checklist for a Good Bitcoin Social Post
- [ ] Hook in first 5 words
- [ ] Mentions BTC/Bitcoin explicitly
- [ ] One clear action (link, DM, reply)
- [ ] Optional: on-chain proof link or stat
- [ ] Relevant hashtags (2–4 max)
- [ ] Under 280 chars for X

## Template A — Product Launch (X/Twitter)
```
We now accept ₿ Bitcoin.

{Product} is live — pay with BTC directly to our wallet.
No fees. No middlemen. {Benefit}.

👉 {short_link}

#Bitcoin #BTC #{YourNiche}
```
