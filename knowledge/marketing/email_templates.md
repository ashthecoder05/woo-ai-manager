# Email Campaign Templates — Bitcoin Commerce

## Checklist for a Good Marketing Email
- [ ] Subject line under 60 chars, with a hook
- [ ] Preview text set (first 90 chars after subject)
- [ ] Personalization in greeting if possible
- [ ] Mentions Bitcoin payment option clearly
- [ ] Single, clear CTA button
- [ ] On-chain proof or trust signal
- [ ] Unsubscribe link (legal requirement)
- [ ] Body 80–600 words

---

## Template 1 — Bitcoin Payment Announcement
**Subject:** We now accept Bitcoin — here's why it matters
**Preview:** Pay directly, no middlemen, instant confirmation.

```
Hi {first_name | there},

Big news: {Store Name} now accepts Bitcoin (BTC).

Why does that matter for you?

✓ No credit card required
✓ Funds go directly to our wallet — no payment processor holds your money
✓ Every payment is verified on the Bitcoin blockchain (publicly auditable)
✓ Works anywhere in the world, no bank account needed

How to pay:
1. Add items to your cart
2. At checkout, select "Pay with Bitcoin"
3. Send BTC to the address shown
4. Your order is confirmed after 2 blockchain confirmations (~20 min)

[Shop now →] {store_url}

Questions? Reply to this email or check our FAQ: {faq_url}

— {Sender Name}, {Store Name}

---
View your payment on-chain: {explorer_url}
Unsubscribe: {unsubscribe_url}
```
